Our Story

From Soweto Kitchen to Maboneng Storefront

The Gvllvnt's Loaf story is a Joburg story. It started in 2020 in a small kitchen in Soweto, where founder Sipho "Gvllvnt" Mthembu began experimenting with flavours from his childhood. His unique creations, shared on social media, quickly caught fire. In 2022, with the support of our community through a crowdfunding campaign, we opened our doors in Maboneng, bringing our township flavour to the city centre.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to disrupt the local bakery scene by creating innovative, high-quality baked goods that celebrate South African flavours, while creating opportunities for aspiring young bakers in our communities.

  • Innovation: We push the boundaries of what bakery can be, with a uniquely South African twist.
  • Community: We believe in lifting as we rise. A portion of our profits goes towards baking workshops for youth in Soweto.
  • Quality: We use the best local ingredients to create world-class products.

Meet The Baker
